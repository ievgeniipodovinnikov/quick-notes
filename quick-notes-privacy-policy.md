# Privacy Policy for Quick Notes

**Last updated:** April 18, 2026

**Version:** 2.0.0

---

## 1. Introduction

Quick Notes is a simple Chrome extension for taking quick notes. This privacy policy explains what data the Extension collects and how it is used.

---

## 2. Data Collection and Storage

Quick Notes does **NOT** collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

All data is stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's `storage.local` API:

### What data is stored:

| Data Type | Description | Purpose |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **Notes** | Text content of your notes | To display and manage your notes |
| **Timestamps** | Date/time when notes were created | To show relative time (e.g., "2 min ago") |
| **Drafts** | Unsaved text in the input field | To restore your text if you accidentally close the popup |
| **Theme preference** | "dark" or "light" mode selection | To remember your theme choice between sessions |

**No data leaves your device.** Everything stays in your browser.

---

## 3. How Your Data Is Used

- **Local storage only:** All notes, drafts, and settings remain on your device
- **No syncing:** Notes are not synced to any cloud service
- **No analytics:** No tracking or usage data collected
- **No external requests:** The extension makes zero network requests

---

## 4. Data Deletion

### Manual deletion:
- Delete individual notes using the × button
- Delete all notes using the "Clear all" button
- Clear drafts using the "Clear" button

### Automatic deletion:
- When you uninstall the extension, **all data is automatically removed** from your device
- Drafts are cleared automatically after saving a note

---

## 5. Permissions

The Extension requests only the `storage` permission to save your notes locally.

**Why only `storage`?**  
Because your privacy matters. No internet access, no tabs, no history — just local storage.

---

## 6. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- **View:** All notes are visible in the Extension
- **Delete:** You can delete individual notes or clear all
- **Export:** Copy any note using the copy button (📋)
- **Remove:** Uninstalling the Extension removes all data

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the "Last updated" date at the top will be updated. Major changes will be noted in the extension's update description on the Chrome Web Store.
